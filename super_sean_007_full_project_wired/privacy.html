Privacy Policy

Last updated: 13 July 2026 · Consent policy version 2026-07-13-v2

Super Sean 007: Legend of the Seven Gems is a free browser game published and operated by Fire Dragon Interactive. No account, real name or email address is required to play. Operator contact details are available through Fire Dragon Interactive.

Local game progress

Your save, settings and consent choice are stored in your browser using localStorage. They remain on that device unless you enable Cloud Sync. You can remove local data through your browser settings. The consent record contains your choice, the policy version and the time of the choice, and is renewed after 180 days or when the policy changes.

Optional Cloud Sync

When you enable Cloud Sync, the game uploads a validated copy of your save to Cloudflare under a random sync ID generated on your device. We do not attach a name, email or account identity to it. Possession of the ID grants access to that save, so treat it like a password.

Cloud saves expire after 180 days of inactivity. Settings lets you download a backup, replace or restore a cloud copy, rotate the sync ID and permanently delete the stored cloud copy.

Aggregate analytics

First-party, cookieless analytics count aggregate events such as page views, new games, battles won, bosses defeated, homestead claims and co-op starts. The current fallback counter is approximate under concurrent traffic. Daily aggregates are retained for up to 120 days. We do not use these events to create individual advertising profiles.

Error diagnostics and service security

When the game encounters an unexpected error, it may send a bounded error message, stack excerpt, page path, code line, release version and current game scene. Reports do not include your save, cloud ID, name, email or raw IP address. The latest 60 reports are retained for no longer than 30 days and can be read only through protected administrator access.

Short-lived SHA-256 network fingerprints may be used only to enforce abuse-prevention limits. Raw addresses are not written to the application’s analytics or diagnostics records.

Advertising and consent

Third-party Adsterra advertising loads only after you choose “Accept advertising.” Choosing “Play without third-party ads” prevents those units from loading. Ad units run inside sandboxed, opaque-origin frames designed to prevent access to game localStorage and cloud IDs. Advertising providers may still process cookies, network data and device information within their own frames under their policies.

You can reset your advertising choice from Settings. Declining advertising does not block normal gameplay, although ad-funded optional rewards are unavailable.

Party Link co-op

Party Link stores short-lived WebRTC signalling records in Cloudflare for approximately ten minutes. Offers and answers are deleted after they are read where possible. Gameplay traffic then travels directly between participating browsers or through an optional TURN relay. Other players may see the display name you enter for the party session.

Service providers and international processing

Cloudflare provides hosting, Functions, KV storage and optional relay configuration. Adsterra provides advertising only after consent. These providers may process data in countries outside your own under their respective terms and transfer safeguards.

Legal bases

Depending on your jurisdiction, optional advertising is based on consent; Cloud Sync and Party Link are processed at your request to provide those features; and limited security, diagnostics and aggregate service measurement are used for operating and protecting the game where permitted by applicable law.

Your choices and rights

  • Decline or reset third-party advertising consent.
  • Keep Cloud Sync disabled and use local saves only.
  • Download, rotate or delete your cloud save from Settings.
  • Clear browser storage to remove local progress, settings, consent and the stored sync ID.
  • Contact the operator to request access, correction, deletion, restriction or objection where those rights apply.
  • Lodge a complaint with your local data-protection authority.

Children

The game is family-friendly and does not request direct identifiers. It is not intended to collect personal profiles from children. Parents and guardians can select the no-ad option and keep Cloud Sync disabled.

Security and contact

Security reports should follow our responsible disclosure policy. General privacy questions can be directed through Fire Dragon Interactive.
