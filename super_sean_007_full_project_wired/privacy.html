Privacy Policy

Last updated: 13 July 2026

Super Sean 007: Legend of the Seven Gems is a free browser game published by Fire Dragon Interactive. No account, real name or email address is required to play.

Local game progress

Your save is stored locally in your browser by default using localStorage. It remains on that device unless you voluntarily enable Cloud Sync. You can erase local progress by clearing browser data or starting a new game.

Optional Cloud Sync

When you enable Cloud Sync, your save is uploaded to Cloudflare against a random sync ID generated on your device. We do not attach your name, email or account identity to that ID. Possession of the sync ID grants access to the save, so treat it like a password. Cloud saves expire after 180 days of inactivity.

Aggregate analytics

We use first-party, cookieless analytics to count aggregate events such as page views, new games, battles won and bosses defeated. Analytics requests are origin checked and rate limited. We do not use analytics cookies, store analytics IP addresses or build advertising profiles from these events.

Error diagnostics and security

If the game encounters an unexpected error, it may send the error message, page path and code line so that we can repair the problem. Error reports do not include your save, name, email or full IP address. Short-lived, hashed network fingerprints may be used solely to enforce abuse-prevention limits on API requests.

Advertising

The game is supported by Adsterra advertising. Third-party ad scripts load only after you accept on the consent banner. Choosing “Play without ad cookies” prevents those scripts from loading during your session. When enabled, advertising partners may use cookies or similar technologies to serve and measure ads. You can change your choice from the in-game Settings menu.

Co-op Party Link

The optional co-op feature uses short-lived Cloudflare records to exchange WebRTC connection information. Signalling records expire after approximately ten minutes; gameplay traffic then travels directly between participating browsers where supported.

Children

The game is family-friendly and does not require personal identifiers. We do not knowingly request or collect names, email addresses, precise locations or account profiles from children.

Your choices

  • Decline third-party ad cookies or reset your decision in Settings.
  • Keep Cloud Sync disabled to retain progress only on your device.
  • Clear browser storage to remove local progress and your stored sync ID.
  • Do not share your cloud sync ID publicly.

Contact

Questions can be directed to Fire Dragon Interactive.
