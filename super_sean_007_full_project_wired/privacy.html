Privacy Policy

Last updated: 11 July 2026

Super Sean 007: Legend of the Seven Gems is a free, browser-based game published by Fire Dragon Interactive. We keep data collection to a minimum. This page explains what happens to your data when you play.

Your game progress

Your save (level, items, homestead, gardens, achievements) is stored locally in your own browser using localStorage. It is not uploaded unless you choose to enable Cloud Sync. You can delete it any time by clearing your browser data or starting a New Game.

Optional cloud sync

If you turn on Cloud Sync, your save is stored on Cloudflare against a random sync ID that is generated on your device. We do not attach your name, email, or any identity to it — possession of the ID is the only key. Saves expire after 180 days of inactivity.

Analytics

We use our own cookieless, first-party analytics. We count aggregate events (such as page views, games started, and bosses defeated) to understand how the game is used. We do not use cookies for analytics, do not store your IP address, and do not build a profile of you. The numbers are anonymous totals.

Advertising

The game is supported by ads from the Adsterra network. Ads load only after you accept on our consent banner. If you choose “Play without ad cookies,” third-party ad scripts are not loaded during your session. When enabled, ad partners may use cookies or similar technologies to serve and measure ads. You can withdraw consent any time from the in-game Settings → “Ad choices”.

Error logging

If the game hits an unexpected error, we may send the error message and the page it happened on so we can fix bugs. This contains no personal data.

Children

The game is family-friendly. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. Because we collect no personal identifiers, no account or personal data is required to play.

Your choices

  • Decline ad cookies on the consent banner, or change your mind in Settings.
  • Clear your browser storage to erase your local save and any sync ID.
  • Play without enabling Cloud Sync to keep everything on your device.

Contact

Questions? Reach the publisher at Fire Dragon Interactive.
