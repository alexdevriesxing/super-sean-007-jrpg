THE STORY SO FAR

One snow day. Two teams. Zero chill.

❄ The First Snow of Maple Hollow

After saving Asteria-007 from Xelar the Bald Wizard, Sean and his friends earned a well-deserved holiday back home — and woke up to the deepest snowfall Maple Hollow has ever seen. School: cancelled. Park: transformed. Plans: enormous.

😤 Enter the Bullies

Rick "The Ringleader" and his eleven-kid crew claimed the whole park before breakfast, planted an orange flag on the east fort and declared themselves Winter Royalty. Sean's squad answered the only honourable way: a blue flag, a west fort, and a formal declaration of snowball war.

🏁 The Rules of Engagement

Two forts. Two flags. First team to three captures owns the park until spring. Everything in between — barricades, snowmen, the frozen lake, even Mr. Henderson's escaped corgi — is fair (and destructible) game.

🐶 The Dog

Nobody knows whose side Biscuit is on. Biscuit does not know whose side Biscuit is on. He remains the single most decorated participant of the conflict.

FIELD GUIDE

Eight power-ups. One frosty park.

Classic Snowball

Six per pack. Five hits sends any kid into the snow. Reloads with a satisfying crunch.

Ice Ball

One perfect frosty hit = an instant 30-second snow nap. Handle with mittens.

Snow Blaster

Twenty rapid-fire shots. Hold down throw and giggle uncontrollably.

Mega Snowball

Three huge lobs with splash damage. Flattens weakened forts and entire ambushes.

Frost Shield

The next three hits shatter harmlessly off a ring of ice. Great for flag runs.

Hot Cocoa

+1 energy and a big speed boost. Grandma-approved battlefield medicine.

Blizzard Bomb NEW

Throws a private snowstorm that batters and slows everyone caught inside for five seconds.

Triple Pack NEW

Your next six throws fan out three-wide. Excellent crowd-control, terrible table manners.

…and keep an eye out for the pink Magnet Mittens, which drag every nearby power-up straight to you for 12 seconds.

THE NEIGHBOURHOOD

Every kid plays differently — speed, strength and aim shape how the squad AI attacks, defends, ambushes and panics. Blue rings are Sean's Squad; orange rings are Rick's bullies.

HOW TO PLAY

Controls & tactics

🖱 Desktop

  • WASD / arrows — move
  • Mouse — aim (pointer lock)
  • Click — throw · hold for the Blaster
  • Right-click — focus: zoom + lead moving targets
  • Shift — sprint · R — reload · Esc — pause

📱 Mobile

  • Left thumb — movement stick appears where you touch
  • Right thumb — drag to aim
  • Big ❄ button — throw · R button — reload
  • Tap ⛶ Fullscreen for the biggest arena
  • Landscape strongly recommended

🧠 Squad tips

  • Orange rings = targets. Blue rings = friends. The dog = neutral chaos.
  • Focus throws predict where a runner will be, not where they are.
  • Mega Snowballs open walls; Blizzard Bombs hold lanes.
  • Ice-bonked bullies nap for 30 seconds — that's a flag run.
  • Low on energy? Cocoa first, heroics second.
