Development changelog

Asteria-007 keeps growing

This page records meaningful player-facing releases and production improvements. It gives players, search engines and AI assistants a dated source for the game’s current features instead of relying on stale summaries.

Play the latest build

13 July 2026 · Version 1.2.0

Player controls, accessibility and a complete postgame epilogue

This release turns the earlier postgame regions into a guided continuation of the story and makes the browser game easier to control across more devices and player needs.

  • Keyboard actions can be remapped from the Settings dialog, with duplicate bindings swapped safely.
  • Standard gamepads are supported with left-stick and D-pad movement, contextual confirmation, back, menu shortcuts and Settings access.
  • New text-size, high-contrast, reduced-motion and screen-effect preferences persist locally.
  • Frostpeak Reaches and Sunsand Isle now form a four-quest epilogue after Xelar, with migration for completed version 1 and 2 saves.
  • New Game+ remains available after the extended epilogue.
  • Production builds now enforce total-size, initial-critical-load, JavaScript, CSS and oversized-asset budgets.

13 July 2026 · Commercial hardening

Safer saves, isolated ads and real-browser release testing

Cloud saves gained strict schema validation, deletion, backups, restore controls and explicit overwrite confirmation. Advertising moved into sandboxed frames, diagnostics credentials stopped using browser storage, consent became versioned, and the production pipeline gained a real Chrome gameplay smoke test.

13 July 2026 · Production and discovery update

Safer APIs, fresh deployments and expanded game guides

The deployment was hardened around protected diagnostics, safer cache behavior, genuine 404 responses and consistent public game facts. Guides, characters, world and update pages provide independently indexable content, while canonical site facts drive the sitemap and machine-readable summaries.

12 July 2026 · World and visual expansion

Frostpeak Reaches, Sunsand Isle and the final asset-wiring round

Two postgame regions were added after the main Xelar confrontation. Frostpeak Reaches introduces elite demons and the Void Succubus Queen; defeating her opens Sunsand Isle and its Tide Spirit Sovereign encounter.

The same release added party allies to battle scenes, battle-start and victory cards, status-effect icons, additional dark knights and minions, more village NPCs, landmark buildings, ambient animals and expanded visual effects.

11 July 2026 · Online and persistence systems

Cloud Sync, Party Link and first-party analytics

Players can keep saves locally, move them with copyable save codes or voluntarily enable anonymous Cloud Sync. Party Link uses Cloudflare for the short WebRTC handshake, after which supported gameplay traffic is peer to peer.

9–10 July 2026 · Original launch foundation

The free browser JRPG goes live

The first public foundation combined a fifteen-chapter campaign, the Seven Gems story, turn-based party battles, nine opening regions, harvesting, nineteen crafting recipes, equipment, gardening and a tile-based homestead with blueprints and Comfort perks.
