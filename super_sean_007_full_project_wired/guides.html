Official player guide

From birthday cake to postgame legend

Super Sean 007 is designed to reward curiosity, but a few smart early choices make the journey smoother. This guide covers your first hour, party recruitment, crafting, the Sunrise Homestead, Cloud Sync and the two postgame regions.

Your first hour in Asteria-007

Talk before wandering

Begin with Elder Brightbeard near the north hall in Birthday Village, then speak to Dave on the plaza. Dave joins immediately and adds Gadget Zap, a reliable stun that buys Sean a safe turn against stronger monsters.

Harvest everything nearby

Press E beside berry hedges, flowers, trees, mushrooms and rocks. Resource nodes take several interactions and later respawn. Wood and stone are especially valuable because they unlock refined materials and the first homestead blueprints.

Open the village chest

The free supplies reduce early grinding. Save consumables for bosses rather than ordinary slimes, and use the first battles to learn Guard, healing and the Friendship meter.

Prepare for Moldor

Mushroom Meadow is the first major region. Defeat the required Slime Sprouts, collect spare materials and approach Moldor with healing items. Guarding before a heavy hit is often stronger than attacking every turn.

Claim Sunrise Homestead

Defeating Moldor awards the land deed. Return to Birthday Village, take the south path and touch the Homestead Crystal. This unlocks build mode, expansion goals and the long-term comfort bonus system.

Build useful rooms first

A cottage is charming, but stations change gameplay. Prioritize a Workbench, Kitchen, Crystal Forge, Storage and Dream Bed. The bed restores the party for free, while Kitchen and Forge recipes support the entire campaign.

Building guide

Build a homestead that helps you win

Refine before decorating

Use Wood and Stone to make Planks and Stone Bricks. Keep a reserve before placing cosmetic pieces so you can still afford stations, equipment and required story builds.

Use blueprints for speed

Press V inside the claimed area to stamp complete structures such as the Cozy Cottage, Stone Keep, Garden Patch, Orchard and Star Shrine. Tile-by-tile mode remains available when you want a custom layout.

Comfort has real effects

Comfort thresholds unlock extra battle XP, a shop discount, attack and defence bonuses and a daily gift chest. A beautiful base is therefore also a practical upgrade path.

Gardens continue growing

Till soil, craft a seed and interact with the plot to plant it. Berry, flower and moonfruit crops use real time, so they can mature while you explore or close the tab.

Remodel without fear

Removing build pieces refunds their materials. Experimenting with rooms, walls and paths does not permanently waste your resources.

Expand the claim

Raise the homestead level to open more of the map. Larger claims make room for specialist stations, gardens, landmark buildings and the animals unlocked by advanced structures.

Equipment priorities

What to craft first

Early healing and refined building materials matter more than collecting every recipe immediately. Berry Juice is a practical safety net, while Mushroom Stew becomes a stronger heal after the Kitchen is ready. The Crystal Sword +1 is an efficient early weapon upgrade; later equipment should be chosen around the party’s current weaknesses rather than rarity alone.

Mine Crystal Cave thoroughly before spending shards, salvage Petro Plains for Gear Parts and collect Moon Herbs whenever you visit the shrine. Keep at least one copy of unusual quest materials until their purpose is clear.

Progress protection

Local saves, save codes and Cloud Sync

The game autosaves locally in your current browser. A copyable save code lets you move progress manually without creating an account. Optional Cloud Sync uploads the save to Cloudflare under a random sync ID, allowing another device to restore it.

  1. Open Settings or the title-screen save tools.
  2. Enable Cloud Sync and copy the generated ID.
  3. Keep the ID private; it works like a password for that anonymous save.
  4. On another device, enable Cloud Sync and enter the same ID.
  5. When warned about a newer cloud save, compare timestamps before forcing an overwrite.

Local browser data can be erased by browser cleanup tools. Keep a save code or sync ID somewhere private before clearing site storage.

After Xelar

Frostpeak Reaches and Sunsand Isle

Frostpeak Reaches

Defeating Xelar opens an icy route beyond the Bald Moon Tower. The region contains high-level demons, rare ore and crystal nodes, the Frozen Cache and the Void Succubus Queen. Defeat her to unlock Sunsand Isle.

Sunsand Isle

The warm postgame shore combines relic hunting with elite corrupted guards and sea creatures. Its final challenge is the Tide Spirit Sovereign, one of the game’s strongest encounters.

Preparation

Enter with upgraded gear, a complete party and a strong supply of healing food. Homestead Comfort bonuses remain valuable, and postgame treasure can support more ambitious building projects.

Combat reference

Status effects and enemy elements

Battles show visible status icons above the health bars, and elemental enemies carry an element icon beside their name. Fire foes can ignite Sean, ice foes chill his attacks and water foes slow skill recovery — so pack snacks and plan turns around the icons you see.
